What's the primary conversion offer?

This is the most important decision — the offer drives revenue. What does a visitor get when they click the CTA?

A

📞 Kostenloses Erstgespräch (Free 30-min call)

Recommended for quick revenue. Book a free strategy call. Low friction, high intent. You qualify leads on the call and close deals directly. Works best with a calendar booking (Calendly etc.).

→ CTA: "Jetzt kostenloses Gespräch buchen"

B

🔍 Kostenloser Automatisierungs-Check (Free Audit)

Visitor fills out a short form → you send a personalized automation audit. Higher perceived value, more info collected upfront. Slightly more friction but warmer leads.

→ CTA: "Meinen kostenlosen Automation-Check starten"

C

💼 Starter-Paket mit Festpreis (Fixed-price offer)

Show a clear €X starter package — e.g. "Erste Automatisierung ab €X". Transparent pricing builds trust and filters serious buyers. Best if you have a repeatable starter service.

→ CTA: "Jetzt Paket anfragen"

D

📊 ROI-Rechner + Beratung (ROI calculator → consultation)

Interactive tool: "How much time & money are you losing to manual tasks?" Calculates a number, then pitches a call. High engagement, educational angle. Slightly more build effort.

→ CTA: "Mein Einsparpotenzial berechnen"
